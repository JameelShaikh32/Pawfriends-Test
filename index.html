About

I lost Kalu, one of my stray dog in May, 2020 since then I couldn't able to see him again! I was broken and had a strong feeling that someone might be taking care of him. I was a Tech student and used to feed around 50 odd dogs and a few cats from my pocket money. I started figuring out how I can find out the lost dogs or how someone can meet their loving friends again.

After searching I found Micro-chip as a solution but those are very expensive and not feasible for everyone to buy and insert into all dogs also it requires a separate scanner to scan the chips and figure out the information.

Taking inspiration from my beloved Kalu, I started coding for a unique identification for all the stray dogs which will be storing all the information about it and we got a QR code to store the information and use it as a tag for the strays so that it can help people to indentity them uniquely and whenever someone will see a lost dog they can findout the owner or care taker of those mute souls.

This QR code will help those mute souls to go back home and meet their parents again!
